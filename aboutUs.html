Understanding

BANKIFY

You account simply, clear, explained.

Every time you open a paid account, a woman is provided with a social account for her.



That account is AND WILL ALWAYS be fully bankrolled for the fees you pay to the bank.



She will also win her first Card Reader completely FREE OF CHARGE, so she can start to engage with her (future) customers in a more professional way and have the possibility of receiving payments via card and online.

Your fees also help to maintain our Business Training For Women platform, which is completely free for social account holders and aims to help them gain even more autonomy for the management and enterprise of small local businesses.
